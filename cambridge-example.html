Translation of compromise – English–Vietnamese dictionary

compromise

 
noun
  /ˈkomprəmaiz/
Add to word list Add to word list
●
(a) settlement of differences in which each side gives up something it has previously demanded
sự thỏa hiệp
We argued for a long time but finally arrived at a compromise.

compromise

 
verb
 
●
to accept less than you originally wanted in order to reach an agreement with someone
dàn xếp
The union is prepared to compromise with the management on the pay issue.
●
to do something which is against your principles or standards
thỏa hiệp
She refused to compromise her principles and accept money from a known criminal.

(Translation of compromise from the PASSWORD English-Vietnamese Dictionary © 2015 K Dictionaries Ltd)
